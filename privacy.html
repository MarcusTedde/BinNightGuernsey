Who we are

Bin Night Guernsey (bins.tedde.tech) is an independent, community-built web application operated by Marcus Tedde. It is not affiliated with the States of Guernsey, Guernsey Waste, or ISL Fusion.

Collection schedule data is sourced live from the official Guernsey Waste calendar at guernsey.isl-fusion.com. We display this data for convenience but cannot guarantee its accuracy ‚Äî always check the official source for confirmation.

What data we collect

We aim to collect as little data as possible. Here is what the app uses:

  • Address searches: When you type an address or postcode to look up your collection schedule, that query is sent via our Cloudflare Worker proxy to the Guernsey Waste calendar. We do not log or store your search queries on our servers.
  • GPS location: If you tap "Use my location", your device's GPS coordinates are sent to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service (a third-party reverse-geocoding service) to identify your postcode. Only the postcode is then used for the address lookup ‚Äî your precise coordinates are not stored by us.
  • Saved addresses: Any addresses you choose to save are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage ‚Äî they remain on your device only and are never sent to our servers.
  • Notification preferences: If you enable collection reminders, your preference is stored in your browser's localStorage on your device only.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to understand aggregate usage (e.g. how many visitors we receive). This does not identify you personally.

Cookies & local storage

This site uses browser localStorage (similar to cookies) to remember your saved addresses and preferences between visits. This data never leaves your device.

If Google AdSense ads are active on this site, Google may set cookies on your device to personalise the ads you see. These are third-party cookies managed by Google, not by us.

Types of cookies used:

  • Essential (localStorage): Stores your saved addresses and reminder settings locally on your device. Required for core app functionality.
  • Analytics cookies (Google): Used to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to serve relevant advertisements. See below for how to manage these.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain, or by using your browser's settings.
